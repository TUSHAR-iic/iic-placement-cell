Institute of Informatics and Communication

University of Delhi South Campus

The Institute of Informatics and Communication, University of Delhi, has evolved into one of the premier institutions of the country since 1997. It targets at fulfilling the increasing demand of trained professionals in the Industry. It has been designed by leading academics in the world of computing as well as communication.

Today, the most recognized need in the world of industry and corporate environment is for an informatics professional who not only understands computing and telecommunication technology but also the software related application. The institute, in its nurturing environment gives birth to and nourishes the mind that go on to innovate in the field of communications and information technology.

Internal Scrutiny

Internal Scrutiny in the Hiring Process

Step 1

Students who are interested to sit for the campus placements register themselves

Step 2

The students are ordered according to their academic performance

Step 3

Online assessments and mock tests are internally conducted for the shortlisted students

Step 4

Students who fail to pass the assessments and mock tests can retake the tests

Recruitment Process

A Step-by-Step Recruitment Process

Students apply for the company placements

Online Assessment(s) are conducted by the company

Interview round(s) are conducted by the company

Interview results are declared and provisional offer letters are granted
